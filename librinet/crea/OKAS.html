Ejercicios de Tiempos Verbales en Inglés

Completa las siguientes frases utilizando el tiempo verbal correcto del verbo entre paréntesis.

Ejercicios

1. My dog always ________ (bark) at the postman.

2. She ________ (not/drink) coffee in the mornings.

3. ________ you ________ (live) in a big city?

4. I ________ (study) for my exam right now.

5. They ________ (not/play) football this afternoon.

6. What ________ he ________ (do) at the moment?

7. We ________ (visit) Rome last summer.

8. He ________ (not/finish) his dinner.

9. ________ she ________ (go) to the party?

10. While I ________ (cook), my sister ________ (watch) TV.

11. They ________ (not/sleep) when I called.

12. What ________ you ________ (do) at 7 PM yesterday?

13. I ________ (never/see) such a beautiful beach.

14. She ________ (not/travel) abroad since 2020.

15. ________ you ever ________ (eat) sushi?

16. He ________ (work) on this project for hours.

17. It ________ (not/rain) much lately.

18. How long ________ they ________ (learn) English?

19. By the time he arrived, we ________ (already/eat).

20. She ________ (not/pack) her bags before the trip.

21. ________ he ________ (leave) when you got there?

22. I ________ (wait) for an hour before the bus came.

23. They ________ (not/practice) enough for the concert.

24. How long ________ you ________ (work) there before you quit?

25. My grandfather ________ (smoke) a pipe.

26. We ________ (not/have) a car when I was young.

27. ________ you ________ (use to/live) near the sea?

28. Every summer, we ________ (would/go) to the mountains.

29. I ________ (wouldn't/miss) my favorite TV show.

30. ________ they ________ (always/play) outside?

31. I think she ________ (pass) the exam easily.

32. He ________ (not/come) to the meeting tomorrow.

33. ________ you ________ (help) me move house?

34. We ________ (meet) our friends for dinner tonight.

35. She ________ (not/study) for her test.

36. ________ they ________ (go to/buy) a new house?

37. This time next year, I ________ (travel) around Europe.

38. I ________ (not/work) when you call me later.

39. ________ you ________ (be/wait) for me at the airport?

40. By 2030, we ________ (build) a new stadium.

41. She ________ (not/finish) her report by Friday.

42. ________ they ________ (have/arrive) before midnight?

43. ________ the children ________ (like) vegetables?

44. The old car ________ (not/start).

45. The baby ________ (sleep) peacefully now.

46. I ________ (not/eat) breakfast yet.

47. I'm sure he ________ (win) the race.

48. We ________ (not/watch) TV tonight.

49. ________ you ________ (listen) to me?

50. By next week, I ________ (not/complete) the entire project.

Soluciones

1. My dog always **barks** at the postman.

2. She **doesn't drink** coffee in the mornings.

3. **Do** you **live** in a big city?

4. I **am studying** for my exam right now.

5. They **are not playing** football this afternoon.

6. What **is** he **doing** at the moment?

7. We **visited** Rome last summer.

8. He **didn't finish** his dinner.

9. **Did** she **go** to the party?

10. While I **was cooking**, my sister **was watching** TV.

11. They **were not sleeping** when I called.

12. What **were** you **doing** at 7 PM yesterday?

13. I **have never seen** such a beautiful beach.

14. She **has not traveled** abroad since 2020.

15. **Have** you ever **eaten** sushi?

16. He **has been working** on this project for hours.

17. It **has not been raining** much lately.

18. How long **have** they **been learning** English?

19. By the time he arrived, we **had already eaten**.

20. She **had not packed** her bags before the trip.

21. **Had** he **left** when you got there?

22. I **had been waiting** for an hour before the bus came.

23. They **had not been practicing** enough for the concert.

24. How long **had** you **been working** there before you quit?

25. My grandfather **used to smoke** a pipe.

26. We **didn't use to have** a car when I was young.

27. **Did** you **use to live** near the sea?

28. Every summer, we **would go** to the mountains.

29. I **wouldn't miss** my favorite TV show.

30. **Would** they **always play** outside?

31. I think she **will pass** the exam easily.

32. He **will not come** to the meeting tomorrow.

33. **Will** you **help** me move house?

34. We **are going to meet** our friends for dinner tonight.

35. She **is not going to study** for her test.

36. **Are** they **going to buy** a new house?

37. This time next year, I **will be traveling** around Europe.

38. I **will not be working** when you call me later.

39. **Will** you **be waiting** for me at the airport?

40. By 2030, we **will have built** a new stadium.

41. She **will not have finished** her report by Friday.

42. **Will** they **have arrived** before midnight?

43. **Do** the children **like** vegetables?

44. The old car **didn't start**.

45. The baby **is sleeping** peacefully now.

46. I **have not eaten** breakfast yet.

47. I'm sure he **will win** the race.

48. We **are not going to watch** TV tonight.

49. **Were** you **listening** to me?

50. By next week, I **will not have completed** the entire project.
