Ejercicios de Tiempos Verbales en Inglés

Completa las siguientes frases utilizando el tiempo verbal correcto del verbo entre paréntesis.

Ejercicios (Mezclados)

1. She usually ________ (go) to bed early.

2. Last summer, we ________ (travel) to Italy.

3. Look! The cat ________ (sleep) on the sofa.

4. They ________ (not/work) on weekends.

5. I ________ (read) an interesting book at the moment.

6. While John ________ (drive) home, he ________ (see) an accident.

7. We ________ (visit) Paris last year.

8. My sister usually ________ (watch) TV in the evenings.

9. I ________ (never/see) such a beautiful sunset.

10. What ________ you ________ (do) this weekend?

11. He ________ (not/eat) breakfast this morning.

12. I think it ________ (rain) tomorrow.

13. At 8 PM yesterday, they ________ (watch) TV.

14. She ________ (live) in Canada since 2010.

15. The sun ________ (rise) in the east.

16. We ________ (have) dinner with friends tonight.

17. Look at those dark clouds! It ________ (snow).

18. Sarah ________ (see) a great movie yesterday.

19. They ________ (travel) to Spain next summer.

20. Don't worry, I ________ (help) you with your homework.

21. The children ________ (play) in the garden when it started to rain.

22. By next year, I ________ (finish) my degree.

23. I ________ (work) all day, so I'm tired now.

24. I ________ (live) in a small town when I was a child.

25. Every summer, we ________ (go) to the beach.

Soluciones

1. She usually goes to bed early.

2. Last summer, we traveled to Italy.

3. Look! The cat is sleeping on the sofa.

4. They don't work on weekends.

5. I am reading an interesting book at the moment.

6. While John was driving home, he saw an accident.

7. We visited Paris last year.

8. My sister usually watches TV in the evenings.

9. I have never seen such a beautiful sunset.

10. What are you doing this weekend?

11. He didn't eat breakfast this morning.

12. I think it will rain tomorrow.

13. At 8 PM yesterday, they were watching TV.

14. She has lived in Canada since 2010.

15. The sun rises in the east.

16. We are having dinner with friends tonight.

17. Look at those dark clouds! It is going to snow.

18. Sarah saw a great movie yesterday.

19. They are going to travel to Spain next summer.

20. Don't worry, I will help you with your homework.

21. The children were playing in the garden when it started to rain.

22. By next year, I will have finished my degree.

23. I have been working all day, so I'm tired now.

24. I used to live in a small town when I was a child.

25. Every summer, we would go to the beach.
